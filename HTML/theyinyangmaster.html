About The Yin Yang Master :

"The Yinyang Master," a 2021 Chinese fantasy film directed by Li Weiran, unfolds the tale of Qing Ming, a Yinyang Master caught between the human and demon worlds. Framed for a guard team's death, Qing Ming escapes and, seven years later, becomes entangled in a plot involving the demon king's impending return.
Released on the first day of the Chinese New Year in 2021 and subsequently on Netflix, "The Yinyang Master" captivates audiences with its rich fantasy elements, intricate plot, and a blend of supernatural action. Starring Chen Kun and Zhou Xun, the film explores the consequences of wielding extraordinary powers and the delicate balance between realms, delivering a visually stunning and emotionally charged cinematic experience.
