About The 8th Night:

"The 8th Night" is a 2021 South Korean mystery-thriller film directed by Kim Tae-hyoung. Distributed by Netflix, the film boasts a runtime of 115 minutes and is presented in the Korean language. The cast includes Lee Sung-min, Park Hae-joon, Kim Yoo-jung, and Nam Da-reum. Released on July 2, 2021, the storyline follows the struggle of a former exorcist attempting to prevent the resurrection of two mysterious beings that tormented humans and were confined in separate caskets for 2500 years.

The plot delves into the ancient history of a monster that caused human suffering, leading to Buddha separating its Red Eye and Black Eye and imprisoning them in Śarīra caskets. In the present, Professor Kim Joon-Cheol seeks to prove the existence of these caskets, setting off a chain of events that involves a former exorcist, a young monk, and a demon seeking resurrection. The narrative unfolds in a night that blurs the lines between reality and the unreal, exploring themes of sacrifice and the battle against supernatural forces. As the characters grapple with their fates and the looming threat of the Red Eye's resurrection, the film takes viewers on a journey filled with suspense, supernatural encounters, and moral dilemmas.
