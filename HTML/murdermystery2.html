About Murder Mystery 2 :

"Murder Mystery 2," directed by Sam Hargrave and written by Joe Russo, is a 2023 American action thriller film that serves as a sequel to the 2020 movie "Extraction." The story follows Tyler Rake, portrayed by Chris Hemsworth, who retired to a cabin in Austria after a harrowing mission in Dhaka. However, he is drawn back into the world of mercenary work when approached by a stranger named Alcott. Tyler is tasked with rescuing his ex-wife Mia's sister, Ketevan, and her two children from the clutches of Davit Radiani, a crime lord imprisoned in Georgia.

As Tyler, along with new recruits Nik and her brother Yaz, infiltrates the prison, chaos ensues, leading to a high-stakes extraction mission and a subsequent series of intense confrontations with criminal forces in Vienna Ultimately, "Murder Mystery 2" builds on the success of its predecessor, offering a compelling narrative within the action thriller genre and leaving room for potential expansion into a cinematic universe, as envisioned by the Russo brothers.
