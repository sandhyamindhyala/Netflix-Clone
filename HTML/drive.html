About Drive

"Drive" is a 2019 Indian Hindi-language action-heist film that follows Vibha Singh, a corrupt bureaucrat, who, along with her team, becomes entangled in a mission to apprehend the notorious criminal King. The plot takes an unexpected turn as undercover agent Irfan Ali infiltrates a street racing gang, only to discover that the mysterious Samar, who gains the gang's trust, is the real King. The film delves into a complex web of betrayal and deception as the characters execute a heist at Rashtrapati Bhavan, leading to unforeseen alliances and a surprising finale.

The cast includes Sushant Singh Rajput as Samar/King, Jacqueline Fernandez as Tara Loha, Vikramjeet Virk as Bikki Kumar, Sapna Pabbi as Naina Sethi, Pankaj Tripathi as Hamid Ibrahim, and Boman Irani as Irfan Ali. Released on Netflix on November 1, 2019, "Drive" marked Sushant Singh Rajput's final film before his untimely death on June 14, 2020.The heist works perfectly well when Irfan, Hamid, and Singh are put off their guard, with Singh being knocked unconscious and her money stolen, but during the mighty escape to the airport, Tara fools Samar and make sure that he is caught by the police, which is when Tara reveals to him that she is King, and it was impulsive for her to betray him for all his smartness. Irfan and Hamid catch up with Samar and arrest him.
