About Viking Wolf:

"Viking Wolf" is a Norwegian horror and thriller film that artfully weaves together historical Viking elements and contemporary horror in a tale spanning centuries. The narrative begins with a Viking raid in 1050 that captures a wolf cub, setting off a chain of supernatural events. Fast-forward to the present day in the small town of Nybo, where teenager Thale, played by Elli Rhiannon Müller Osborne, becomes a central figure in the town's struggle against a mysterious and menacing force.

As the plot unfolds, Thale's connection to the supernatural occurrences becomes increasingly apparent, drawing her and her mother, Liv, played by Liv Mjönes, into a web of ancient curses and contemporary struggles. The film introduces a mysterious one-armed man named Lars, who claims to have spent his life tracking a werewolf. The climax brings a confrontation with the werewolf, leaving the fate of the characters open-ended and adding to the film's suspenseful and captivating narrative.
