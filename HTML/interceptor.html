About Interceptor:

"Interceptor," a 2022 action thriller film directed by Matthew Reilly in his directorial debut, is an international co-production between Australia and the United States. The film stars Elsa Pataky and Luke Bracey and centers around U.S. Army Captain J. J. Collins, who, after being wrongfully ousted from her dream job at the Pentagon, finds herself in charge of a remote nuclear missile interceptor base in the Pacific Ocean. The narrative unfolds as Collins becomes the last line of defense against a terrorist faction's hostile takeover of a crucial U.S. interceptor launch site.

The film is noted for its action sequences, tense narrative, and Elsa Pataky's performance. As Collins faces personal and professional challenges, the film culminates in a gripping finale, highlighting her determination and courage in the face of adversity. The resolution sees Collins honored for her efforts, providing a satisfying conclusion to the intense action thriller.. cinematic universe, as envisioned by the Russo brothers.
