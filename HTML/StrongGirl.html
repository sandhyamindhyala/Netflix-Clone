About Strong Girl Nam-soon:

Strong Girl Nam-soon is a 2023 South Korean television series starring Lee Yoo-mi in the title role, along with Kim Jung-eun, Kim Hae-sook, Ong Seong-wu, and Byeon Woo-seok. The series serves as a spin-off to the 2017 series Strong Girl Bong-soon. It aired on JTBC from October 7 to November 26, 2023, every Saturday and Sunday at 22:30 (KST).[10] It is also available for streaming on Netflix in selected regions.The series received audience acclaim, with its final episode recording 10.42% in nationwide ratings.
