About No One Gets Out Alive:

"No One Gets Out Alive" is a 2021 British horror film directed by Santiago Menghini, based on the 2014 novel of the same name by Adam Nevill. The movie follows Ambar, an undocumented immigrant from Mexico, who moves to Cleveland, Ohio, after her mother's death. Settling into a dilapidated boarding house run by Red, Ambar experiences supernatural occurrences and encounters ghostly figures, leading her to unravel a dark mystery. In her quest for a better life, she faces betrayal, financial struggles, and the disturbing secrets of her boarding house's past.

The film explores themes of immigration, sacrifice, and the supernatural, blending horror elements with a compelling narrative. As Ambar delves deeper into the mysteries of the house, she discovers a horrifying ritual connected to a stone box. The climax involves a nightmarish sequence in which Ambar faces the Aztec goddess Ītzpāpālōtl, tied to a moth-like creature. "No One Gets Out Alive" provides a gripping and atmospheric horror experience with its unique blend of cultural mythology and psychological terror.
