About Heart Of Stone :

"Heart of Stone" is a 2023 American spy action thriller film directed by Tom Harper, featuring a screenplay by Greg Rucka and Allison Schroeder, with a story by Rucka. The film stars Gal Gadot, Jamie Dornan, Alia Bhatt, Sophie Okonedo, and Matthias Schweighöfer. It was released on August 11, 2023, by Netflix. The plot follows an international intelligence operative (played by Gal Gadot) who embarks on a perilous mission to protect a mysterious artificial intelligence system known as "The Heart." Tasked by the peacekeeping operation called Charter, she must ensure the safety of this object to prevent it from falling into the wrong hands.

Filming for "Heart of Stone" took place in Europe from January to July of the release year, and it was ultimately distributed by Netflix. In the storyline, the protagonist operates in various locations, including the Italian Alps and London, as she navigates complex scenarios involving MI6 field agents, a hacker named Keya Dhawan, and the pursuit of The Heart, a quantum computer guiding Charter's missions. The film explores themes of espionage, betrayal, and the quest to safeguard powerful technology. The cast includes notable names like Gal Gadot, Jamie Dornan, Alia Bhatt, Sophie Okonedo, and Matthias Schweighöfer, contributing to the film's blend of action and intrigue.
