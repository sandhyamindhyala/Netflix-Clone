About Believer 2 :

"Believer 2" is a 2023 South Korean action crime film and a sequel to the 2018 film "Believer." Directed by Baek Jong-yul, the movie stars Cho Jin-woong, Cha Seung-won, Han Hyo-joo, Oh Seung-hoon, Kim Dong-young, and Lee Joo-young. The storyline revolves around Detective Jo Won-ho's pursuit of "Rak," who vanished after Brian's imprisonment, while uncovering the secrets of a mysterious drug cartel. The film premiered at the 28th Busan International Film Festival on October 5, 2023, and became available for streaming on Netflix from November 17, 2023.

The main cast includes Cho Jin-woong as Jo Won-ho, Cha Seung-won as Brian Lee, and Han Hyo-joo as Big Knife, along with other supporting characters. The sequel has received mixed reviews, with some critics praising the film for its intricate plot and twists, while others criticize the excessive violence and the introduction of a preposterous new villain, Big Knife. Despite varying opinions, the film maintains a level of intensity and histrionic mayhem, offering a continuation of the storyline from its predecessor.
