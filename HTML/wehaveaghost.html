About We Have A Ghost:

We Have a Ghost" is a 2023 American supernatural horror comedy film written and directed by Christopher Landon, inspired by Geoff Manaugh's 2017 short story "Ernest." The movie features an ensemble cast, including David Harbour, Jahi Winston, Tig Notaro, Jennifer Coolidge, and Anthony Mackie. Released on February 24, 2023, by Netflix, the film received mixed reviews from critics.

The storyline follows the Presley family, who purchase an abandoned house with a resident ghost named Ernest, discovered by the youngest family member, Kevin. Eager to help Ernest uncover his mysterious past, Kevin befriends his neighbor Joy. They embark on a journey to Oklahoma to find information about Ernest's previous owner, Ernest S. The plot takes a supernatural turn as it delves into the history of the ghostly figure and involves a CIA program for capturing ghosts, led by horror writer Dr. Leslie Monroe.The film concludes with the Presleys moving to a new house, Kevin and Joy becoming a couple, and a mysterious flicker of light in the attic hinting at the possibility of more supernatural encounters.
