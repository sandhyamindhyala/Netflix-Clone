About The Mother :

"The Mother," a 2023 American action thriller directed by Niki Caro, features Jennifer Lopez as a former U.S. military operative, referred to as "the Mother." The film unfolds as she navigates a complex web of criminality involving arms smuggling, child trafficking, and a romantic entanglement with ex-SAS Captain Adrian Lovell and arms dealer Hector Álvarez. After becoming pregnant and discovering the criminal activities of her associates, the Mother becomes an informant for the FBI.

Twelve years later, when her daughter, Zoe, is kidnapped by Álvarez's men, the Mother partners with FBI Special Agent William Cruise to rescue her. The narrative unfolds across Ohio, Havana, and a remote cabin in Alaska, exploring themes of sacrifice, survival, and the complexities of maternal love. Despite casualties and sacrifices, including the death of Cruise, the Mother successfully defends her daughter and ultimately returns Zoe to her adoptive family.
