About Sister Death:

In the aftermath of the vengeful spirit's actions, Narcisa is found by the priest and is questioned about the recent events. She reveals the truth about Socorro's tragic past and how she helped free her spirit. Despite the chaos, Narcisa chooses to stay at the convent and continue her duties. The film concludes with a sense of closure as Narcisa seems to have come to terms with her supernatural abilities and has made peace with the spirits haunting the convent.

"Sister Death" explores themes of trauma, revenge, and redemption against the backdrop of post-war Spain. Directed by Paco Plaza, known for his work in the horror genre, the film combines supernatural elements with historical context to create a chilling and atmospheric narrative. The intertwining of religious imagery, paranormal activities, and the traumatic aftermath of war adds layers to the storyline, making it a compelling and haunting experience for viewers. The film's release on Netflix provides a global audience with the opportunity to delve into the supernatural mysteries and horrors of "Sister Death."
