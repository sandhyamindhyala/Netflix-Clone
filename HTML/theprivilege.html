About The Privilege:

"The Privilege" (German: Das Privileg) is a 2022 German film directed by Felix Fuchssteiner and Katharina Schöne. This thriller, released by Netflix on February 9, 2022, boasts a runtime of 107 minutes and unfolds in the German language. The film's screenplay, crafted by Felix Fuchssteiner, Sebastian Niemann, Katharina Schöde, and Eckhard Vollmar, weaves a narrative featuring a cast led by Max Schimmelpfennig, Lise Risom Olsen, and Caroline Hartig.

The storyline revolves around characters such as Yvonne Bergmann, portrayed by Lise Risom Olsen, and Anna, played by Caroline Hartig. As the plot unfolds, it introduces additional characters like Lena (Lea van Acken), Agent Trondthal (Dieter Bach), and Samira (Tijan Marei). With its release on Netflix, "The Privilege" offers audiences a suspenseful cinematic experience, blending elements of thriller and drama against a German backdrop.
